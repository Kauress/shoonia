FAQ

Want to ask something not answered here?

Contact me here

What program should I enroll for?

For absolute beginners I suggest the Python or Web development tracks. After a brief introduction I will suggest a course track that aligns with your child's interest. The advantage of small classes is that programs can be scaled up and down according to an individual.

Is there an age limit?

The lower age limit is 9 years.

How will you teach my child?

I will teach using Zoom and an online platform where both parties can see and work on code at the same time. I will also use coding worksheets and projects.

How many students in a class?

O Small classes (upto 5 learners in 1 class). This ensures I can give individual attention to each learner.

Do you offer a satisfacton guarantee?

Yes, I offer a full money-back guarantee within your first 2 classes.

I'm unsure if my child will get/understand coding?

I have been teaching coding for 7+ years, it is my job to make sure that your child understands coding concepts. After an intial call and the 1st 2-3 classes I will know how your child learns best and her learning style and pace.

Do you offer a trial class?

Yes, after an initial call/email there is a trial class that you can sign up for.

Do I need to buy anything?

No, unless your child is learning Virtual Reality, in which case you will need a VR headset (Googlr cardboard is also fine)

My child has autism/speacial learning needs. Can they still learn?

Yes, I will need to take them through 2-3 classes to see how they interact with words/colors and images and then scale the curriculum to their pace and strengths.

Are the classes theory or project based learning?

We will do theory and practical work. A solid grounding in theory will make practical work more interactive and fun. Children can choose what project they want to make, it will be entirely their idea.
